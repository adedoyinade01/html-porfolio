Top 3 Movies Of All Time.

Ready Player One

The best sci-fi movie i can recommend.

Gives a touch of virtual reality and also a gaming kind of like experience.

Naruto Shippuden

This is by far the best anime series without doubt.

Naruto is a story of determination, perseverance, and the enduring belief in one's ability to make a positive change in the world.

And also emphasies on the power of friendship.

The Avengers

They have the best storylines for the superheroes/Action genre.
